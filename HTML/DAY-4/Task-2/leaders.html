Political leaders travelled throughout Tamil Nadu and addressed thousands of people during election rallies and public meetings. Their speeches focused on development, welfare schemes, education, employment, and industrial growth.

M. K. Stalin continued to promote the achievements and welfare programs implemented by the current government. He highlighted infrastructure development, education schemes, and healthcare improvements during his campaign speeches.

Edappadi K. Palaniswami focused on administrative experience and development projects completed during the previous AIADMK government. He promised additional welfare programs and economic growth plans for the state.

Vijay, the founder of TVK, attracted large numbers of youth supporters across Tamil Nadu. His public meetings became highly popular and social media platforms were filled with campaign updates.

Annamalai and Seeman also conducted energetic campaigns and discussed topics such as nationalism, agriculture, youth development, and state rights.
