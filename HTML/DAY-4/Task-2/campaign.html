Election campaigns in Tamil Nadu were conducted with great energy and excitement. Political parties organized rallies, roadshows, public meetings, and social media promotions across different districts.

Campaign events attracted huge crowds in Chennai, Madurai, Coimbatore, Salem, Trichy, and several other cities. Supporters gathered in large numbers to listen to speeches delivered by political leaders.

Major campaign topics included employment opportunities for youth, education improvements, infrastructure projects, healthcare facilities, industrial growth, and women welfare schemes.

Social media became one of the most important tools for election campaigns. Political parties used YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and X (Twitter) to reach young voters and share campaign updates.

Television debates, interviews, and public discussions also influenced voters during the election season. Media channels provided continuous updates regarding rallies, speeches, and public opinion surveys.
