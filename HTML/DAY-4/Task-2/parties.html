Political parties in Tamil Nadu play a major role in shaping the state's administration, economy, and public welfare policies. Every party released separate election manifestos explaining their development plans and promises.

DMK focused mainly on social welfare schemes, education support, women welfare programs, and industrial development projects. The party highlighted the achievements of the current government.

AIADMK concentrated on administrative experience, infrastructure development, and public welfare schemes. The party promised new economic growth initiatives and employment opportunities.

TVK gained attention because of actor Vijay’s entry into politics. The party attracted many young voters and promoted transparency, youth empowerment, and social development.

BJP, NTK, and PMK also conducted active campaigns across the state and discussed topics such as agriculture, education, state rights, industrial growth, and public safety.
